What is Fermium LABS?

We aren't opened every day from 9am to 6pm. We haven't got project managers with 30 years of experience, quality consultants or human resource management teams.

We're a small, tight-knit teams of broad backgrounds guided by the ingenuity of way too young minds.

In Fermium LABS srl, products are born directly within new technologies. We rethink, reinvent and redesign what's been left behind
